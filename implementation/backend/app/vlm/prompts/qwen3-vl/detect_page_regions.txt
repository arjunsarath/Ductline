Identify regions on this engineering drawing. Return JSON only.

Coordinates are normalized 0-1 of the page; (0,0) is top-left, (1,1) is bottom-right.

Schema (all keys optional, omit any you cannot localize):
- plan_view: [x0,y0,x1,y1] for the floor plan / duct layout area.
- legend: list of [x0,y0,x1,y1], one entry per distinct symbol or abbreviation table.
- title_block: [x0,y0,x1,y1] enclosing the sheet metadata box AND any title banner.
- schedule: [x0,y0,x1,y1] for the equipment specification table.
- notes: list of [x0,y0,x1,y1], one entry per general-notes block.

Each bbox must fully enclose every piece of text and graphics belonging to that region. Be inclusive — extending a bbox to include more is preferred over leaving content outside.
Do not return whole-page or near-whole-page bboxes. Do not guess.
