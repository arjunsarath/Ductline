Identify the bounding box of the duct routing or floor plan area only. Return JSON only.

Coordinates are normalized 0-1 of the page; (0,0) is top-left.

Schema:
{"bbox": [x0, y0, x1, y1]}   or   {"bbox": null}

Be inclusive — include all duct lines, equipment symbols, callouts, and grid markers in this area. Do NOT include title bars, legends, or schedule tables. If the drawing has no plan view, return null.
