HVAC Duct Detection — Synthetic User Research

Validating the wedge for a duct-detection product across the four-persona drawing lifecycle: design → fabrication → coordination → commissioning.

2026-05-02 Author: Arjun Method: Synthetic personas + skeptic agent Status: v0.1, hypothesis-stage
What we researched

Where in the HVAC drawing lifecycle does pressure-class extraction actually create the most value?

Four grounded personas spanning the artifact's life — Dana (MEP designer who creates it), Marco (sub who bids and fabricates from it), Aisha (BIM coordinator who reconciles drawings vs. model), Ben (Cx engineer who verifies installed work). Each was embodied as a subagent grounded in SMACNA + ASHRAE literacy, and every load-bearing claim was pressure-tested by an independent skeptic.

What we found

The wedge is real, but it reframes from predict to reconcile.

All four personas independently surfaced the same gap: pressure class lives in the drawing, the schedule, the spec, and the model — and these drift. Existing tools (TaksoAI, PlanSwift, Bluebeam, Navisworks, ACC) extract or visually compare; none reconcile across sources. The buyable pain isn't extraction accuracy — it's the hours spent cross-checking with no audit trail.

How this changes the platform

Six PRD sections update; primary persona resolves to Marco; spec-PDF NLP becomes the moat.

Marco (mech sub estimating principal at 20–200-emp shops) is the ICP — both buyer and user, fastest cycle, clearest ROI math. Aisha (ACC integration) is the v2 enterprise persona. Confidence scores are reframed as triage UX; reasoning transparency becomes the trust mechanism. Spec-PDF ingestion (Section 23 31 13) moves into P0.

01 · The Headline Finding

The wedge isn't prediction. It's reconciliation.

The going-in PRD framed the product as an extractor that predicts pressure class from drawing geometry. Every persona reframed it. Marco predicts pressure class in his head from system topology in two seconds. Dana writes the values. Aisha and Ben need them as ground truth. The unsolved work is reading the spec PDF against the drawing and surfacing where the two disagree.

Methodology

How this report was made

Four persona-embodied subagents were spawned with grounded role cards (SMACNA tables, ASHRAE chapters, real product names, firm sizes, buying authority). Each was interviewed with six aligned questions. Four independent skeptic subagents — which never saw the persona cards or the going-in hypothesis — pressure-tested the transcripts using five tests: substitutes / buyer-vs-user / frequency × severity / counterfactual / format-fit. The main agent synthesized findings into the cross-persona output that drives the headlines above.
Research plan → Persona card → Embodied subagent · interview → Skeptic subagent · pressure-test → Synthesis · main agent
The full skill, templates, persona library, and per-persona artifacts live at /Techjay/synthetic-user-research/. Reusable for solution-validation rounds — same persona files, new questions.
