## Hypothesis

The single load-bearing belief we want to test. Should be falsifiable.

> Example: "Pressure-class extraction is the highest-value gap in HVAC duct takeoff because TaksoAI and PlanSwift handle quantity but require manual pressure-class reconciliation, and the chronic conservative gauge padding when pressure class is ambiguous costs sub-contractors win-rate more than rare catastrophic misreads."

## Research questions

Five to eight. Avoid yes/no. Each question should be answerable in concrete terms by the persona.

1. Walk me through your last takeoff. Where does pressure class come from on the drawing?
2. When the drawing is ambiguous on pressure class, what do you actually do?
3. What's the cost — to you, to the bid — of that ambiguity?
4. What tools have you tried? What did each not solve?
5. If extraction was 90% accurate, would you stop verifying? Why / why not?
6. Who buys software at your firm, and what's the threshold where it goes from your discretionary spend to a board-level decision?
